Useful Sites

## USEFUL SITES

THE WIKI

[https://oxygennotincluded.wiki.gg/](https://oxygennotincluded.wiki.gg/wiki/Oxygen_Not_Included_Wiki)

The wiki has information about dupes, resources, critters, geysers, asteroids, and pretty much everything else you can find in the game.

PROFESSOR OAKSHELL'S LAB

<https://zari.rtk0.net/ProfessorOakshell/index.html>

Includes a bunch of useful calculators, including food, farming, heat and rockets.

(TOOLS NOT INCLUDED)

[(https://toolsnotincluded.net)](https://toolsnotincluded.net/map-tools/map-browser)

Tools Not Included lets you browse maps to help you find one with exactly the kinds of geysers and planets you're looking for. Sadly, the site has been down for a while but there is hope it will be back.

ONI ASSISTANT

[https://oni-assistant.com](https://oni-assistant.com/)

The Oni Assistant website has lots of useful tools, including a food calculator and a calculator to figure out how much fuel you will need for your rocket (in the base game).

THE DEVELOPER'S FORUMS

[https://forums.kleientertainment.com](https://forums.kleientertainment.com/)

On Klei's forums you can read about updates to the game, learn about mods, submit a suggestion or a bug report, and more.

REDDIT

[https://www.reddit.com/r/Oxygennotincluded](https://www.reddit.com/r/Oxygennotincluded/)

Reddit's Oxygen Not Included subforum is a good place to talk about the game, see builds, or ask questions about your own.

DISCORD

<https://discord.gg/EBncbX2>

The Oxygen Not Included discord has announcements, information about game updates, discussions and more.

YOUTUBE

Various channels. Some also include tutorials.

Some examples: [Anarod](https://www.youtube.com/@Anarod), [Blitz After Dark](https://www.youtube.com/c/BlitzAfterDark), [Brothgar](https://www.youtube.com/user/Brothgar), [Cryptic Fox](https://www.youtube.com/user/CrypticFoxGaming), [Echo Ridge Gaming](https://www.youtube.com/c/EchoRidgeGaming), [Francis John](https://www.youtube.com/user/MrFranciscraven), [GCFungus](https://www.youtube.com/@GCFungus), [Grind This Game](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC6STTjHxnSXp-r8NPQIutAQ), [Luma plays](https://www.youtube.com/c/Lumaplays1), [Magnet](https://www.youtube.com/user/xunlucky17x), [Mullematsch](https://www.youtube.com/c/Mullematsch/), [Nathan's Sandbox](https://www.youtube.com/c/RedstoneNightmare)[,](https://www.youtube.com/c/RedstoneNightmare) [Nilaus](https://www.youtube.com/c/Nilaus), [QuantumZari](https://www.youtube.com/@QuantumZari), [Skye Storme](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLbCLzFKeiWkM69zrZKMIO0VB_vc6sPMbU) and [Toni Advanced](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC3LfCJ2vQi-vSP6JXLG6l5w) (as well as Josh's ONI antics at [Let's Game it Out](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qIuRghe1EZU))

About

## ABOUT

Hiya, I'm some random Finn. If I told you how many thousands of hours of Oxygen Not Included I have played over the years, you might be tempted to think two things: that I know what I'm doing (I don't), and that I have learned the designs I use often by heart (I haven't).

The resources, guides and builds put together by the community have been a huge help in learning how to piece together an acceptable existence for my dupes. I created this site to give back to that wonderful community. Officially, anyway.

Really, I created this site so I don't have to repeatedly spend hours clicking around in YouTube videos looking for build settings and overlays. (But if anyone asks, tell them the community thing.)

![FinalTypewriterClear.png](assets/FinalTypewriterClear-05c5a435f5.png)

Woooot - I'm a dupe! (Want to be one, too? Talk to [Draken](https://www.deviantart.com/drakenshaven/gallery/64082208/oxygen-not-included-fanart):-)
