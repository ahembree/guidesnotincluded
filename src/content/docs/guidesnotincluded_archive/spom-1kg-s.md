SPOM, OXYGEN: 1KG/S

This is Francis John's take on the classic SPOM, modified to fit in a four-tile high area.

Atmo sensor, upper (hydrogen): above 750

Atmo sensor, lower (oxygen): above 450

Note: use gold (or better) for things can can overheat. The upper section can be fully enclosed, the hydrogen cools the top level.

A not on sources: the SPOM was first introduced to the world by QuQuasar in [a post on Klei's forums](https://forums.kleientertainment.com/forums/topic/87548-self-powering-oxygen-module-mkii-production-and-cooling/). Francis John noted that the design shown here was inspired by a version he saw Cryptic Fox build.
